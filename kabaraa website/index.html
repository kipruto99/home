Who are The Kabara?

Our main ancestor, Mzee Kibara is our Patriarch. We belong to the clan called KIPKOIITIM, and the Elephant - Peliot is our totem.
Our ancestors migrated to Nandi from Mt Elgon and Segela Masai.
